Restaurants are loud, fast, and full of small decisions. The people closest to the guest see things none of us can. That's the whole point. Our restaurants only work because of the brilliant people in them, every shift, every station, every day. We call it Collective Genius for a reason. None of us, not me, not any single manager, is going to out-think a restaurant full of cooks, bartenders, servers, and hosts who are living the work in real time. You notice the thing that's off. You have the idea the rest of us haven't had yet. So I'm asking, plainly: tell us. The good, the broken, the half-baked, the thing you almost didn't bother mentioning.

— Mike
